The B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Bombay is a 4-year undergraduate program that provides comprehensive education in computer science fundamentals, software development, and emerging technologies. The program emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical applications through laboratory work and projects.

Candidates must have completed their 10+2 education with at least 75% aggregate marks (65% for SC, ST, and PwD categories) from a recognized board. Admission is primarily based on the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Advanced rank. Prospective students must first qualify for JEE Main to be eligible for JEE Advanced.

The total tuition fee for the B.Tech program is approximately ₹8,00,000.
One-time Admission Fee: ₹16,000
Hostel Fees: Approximately ₹2,94,200.
IIT Bombay offers various scholarships: Merit-based Scholarships, Need-based Financial Assistance and Government Scholarships.
